The PDFs in the following folder consists of the handwritten notes that describe key ideas from the respective chapter.
These notes are based on the book Quantum Computation and Quantum Information by Michael A. Nielsen and Isaac L. Chuang.
Note that it is important to read the book first to understand the concepts completely.
This is one of the best books to help you get started with Quantum Computing.
If you have gone through the book or want a rough idea about the topics, go through these PDFs.
